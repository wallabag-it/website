## Instapaper: a good idea with real limitations

Instapaper is one of the pioneers of read-it-later. Since its launch in 2008, it has allowed users to save articles and read them later on any device, often offline. Its reading interface is clean and it has a loyal user base.

That said, Instapaper has limitations that are hard to ignore. Its best features (full-text search, unlimited highlights, text-to-speech) are locked behind the Premium subscription, priced at €55/year. The free tier still works but is noticeably cut down. The app has also had an unsettled history: acquired by Pinterest in 2016, then sold off again in 2018, leaving its long-term future uncertain.

## Some alternatives

Several services offer a read-it-later experience:

- Readwise Reader is feature-rich (RSS, highlights, notes, sync) but its pricing, around €96/year, makes it a tool for the most committed users.
- Matter was a well-regarded app for its design, but its development has been put on hold.
- wallabag.it is an open-source alternative, hosted in Europe, that covers the essentials without the inflated pricing.

## Meet wallabag.it

wallabag.it is the hosted version of wallabag, an open-source read-it-later app. It was built around one simple idea: **your reading list should belong to you**.

### Full article storage

Unlike tools that only save a link or partial content, wallabag fetches the full text of the page, cleaned up and ad-free. Even if the original website disappears, your copy stays intact.

### Privacy by design

wallabag does not track you, sell your data or clutter your reading with ads. It is open-source software, run by people who believe in user freedom.

### Hosted in Europe

All data on wallabag.it is stored on servers located in Europe, protected by strict EU privacy laws and fully GDPR-compliant.

### Offline reading

Sync your saved articles across all your devices (phones, tablets and even e-readers like Kobo) and read them without a connection.

### Tags, annotations and search

Organise your articles with tags, take notes, and quickly find past reads with full-text search. All these features are included in the base subscription, at no extra cost.

### Easy migration from Instapaper

[Leaving Instapaper is straightforward](https://helpdesk.wallabag.it/help/en-gb/2-import/11-how-to-migrate-from-instapaper-to-wallabag-it): wallabag.it provides an import tool that preserves your articles and their metadata.
