## Matter: a well-designed app with a specific focus

Matter has built a loyal audience for its clean, minimal reading experience and its handling of newsletters alongside articles. It is particularly appreciated among iOS users who want a focused, distraction-free reading environment.

That said, Matter has some real limitations if your needs go beyond that specific use case: it is iOS-first, US-hosted and proprietary. If you use Android, own a Kobo e-reader, or care about where your data is stored, you will quickly run into its edges.

## Some alternatives

- Readwise Reader is the most feature-complete option in the space — RSS, highlights, AI, notes — but at €96/year and US-hosted, it is a big commitment.
- Instapaper is a long-standing read-it-later app at €55/year, with highlights and notes, though its ownership history is uncertain.
- wallabag.it is the open-source, EU-hosted alternative that covers the core reading use case on every platform, at €11/year.

## Meet wallabag.it

wallabag.it is the hosted version of wallabag, an open-source read-it-later app running since 2013. It was built around one idea: **your reading list should belong to you**.

### Full article storage

wallabag fetches the full text of every page you save, cleaned up and ad-free. Even if the original website disappears, your copy stays intact.

### Privacy by design

wallabag does not track you, sell your data or show you ads. It is open-source software, run by people who believe in user freedom rather than exploitation.

### Hosted in Europe

All data on wallabag.it is stored on servers located in Europe, protected by strict EU privacy laws and fully GDPR-compliant — a meaningful difference compared to US-based services.

### Android, iOS and e-readers

wallabag has fully-featured native apps for both Android and iOS, with offline reading. It also connects directly to Kobo e-readers. Your reading list follows you everywhere, regardless of which devices you use.

### Tags, annotations and search

Organise your articles with tags, take notes, and find past reads quickly with full-text search. Every feature is included in the base subscription.
