## Readeck: a strong choice if you want to self-host

Readeck is one of the most interesting read-it-later projects to appear in recent years. It is open source, modern, and built for people who want to run their own service.

If you enjoy maintaining a server, backups, updates and monitoring, Readeck can be a very good option.

But not everyone wants another service to administer.

## The real question: self-hosting or hosted service?

For many people leaving Pocket or Omnivore, the choice is not only between two apps. It is between two responsibilities:

- self-hosting, where you control the server and also maintain it;
- a hosted service, where you pay a small subscription and someone else keeps it running.

wallabag.it is for the second case. You keep the benefits of an open-source read-it-later tool, but without running the infrastructure yourself.

## Where wallabag.it fits

wallabag.it is the hosted version of wallabag. It is built around the same simple idea as the open-source project: save articles, clean them up, read them later, and keep control of your reading list.

### Hosted in Europe

Your wallabag.it account is hosted in Europe and operated by the wallabag team. You do not need to configure Docker, updates, backups or email delivery.

### Open source, but no server work

You can self-host wallabag if you want full infrastructure control. Or you can use wallabag.it if you want the service maintained for you.

### Reading across devices

Save articles from your browser, read on mobile, export, search, tag, annotate, and keep reading offline. wallabag.it also works well with e-readers such as Kobo.

### Sustainable pricing

wallabag.it starts at €11/year. The subscription funds the hosted service and the ongoing maintenance of the project.

## Quick comparison

| Service         | Best for                                      | Main trade-off                                      |
| --------------- | --------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Readeck         | People who want to self-host a modern app     | You maintain the server yourself                    |
| wallabag self-hosted | People who want full control of wallabag | You maintain the server yourself                    |
| **wallabag.it** | People who want hosted read-it-later in Europe | Paid subscription, starting at €11/year             |

## Final thoughts

If your priority is running your own stack, Readeck deserves a look. If your priority is reading later without maintaining yet another service, wallabag.it is the easier choice.
